Laziza a taste of Lebanon- Est 2010

Traditional food from the eastern Mediterranean.

Lebanese cuisine is a food culture that has been inherited from one generation to another. The aroma of a homemade stew, the taste of hummus and a freshly baked pita bread is something that I and many of my employees in Laziza have grown up. It has created a community and a pride that we want to share with us. Welcome to a culinary trip from Beirut to Malmö - Lebanese food at its best!
